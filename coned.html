#CONED - MFA Thesis Project

A critical look into how our personal electronic devices have shaped us physically, #CONED raises awareness about the issue of poor posture. "Texting neck" is the position we are in when using our phones, adding sixty extra pounds onto our spines. We can only imagine what that posture will look like in years to come if we continue to look down. #CONED is the necessary awareness to help us start looking up!
