Here's a brief history of who I am, what I do, and why we should be friends or even work together... I am originally from Dallas, Texas and it was Junior year of high school that my creative talents truly emerged. I developed a passion and curiosity for Graphic Design, which would lead me to a Bachelor's of Fine Arts degree in Visual Communications from the University of Arizona.

2014 was life-changing. I had one degree down, but it was then that I realized I just wasn't ready to stop learning. I was interested in expanding my talents to a broader creative voice.

The applications were submitted and the waiting game was endless. It was then, while on a trip to Chicago that I received the call that would change everything. I would be moving to New York to continue my education at one of the most prominent design schools in the country, Parsons The New School. August 2015 I would start my two year Master's journey in the Design and Technology program.

The two years flew by. My experience at Parsons was incredible. The curriculum, faculty, and especially my fellow classmates challenged myself to grow as a designer. While many of my projects represent my learning and passion for product development and design, I have really learned that there are so many areas to tackle that will only help myself further my career. I love what I do and it only helps me continue to expand my creativity.
